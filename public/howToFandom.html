How to Fandom

Do your research: Familiarize yourself with the content related to your interest, whether it be watching all episodes of a TV show, reading novels, or playing video games. Joining fandoms can lead to lasting friendships and a wholesome fan experience!

Get involved online: Look for online communities dedicated to your fandom. Websites such as Reddit, Tumblr, and Facebook often have dedicated groups or subreddits.
