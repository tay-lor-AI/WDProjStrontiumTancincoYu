Hermitcraft SMP

Overview

Hermitcraft is a survival multiplayer series in Minecraft where a group of talented Minecraft creators play together in 1 world. Typically, their definition of having fun is creating long-term projects like massive, intricately detailed bases or very technical builds like minigames, or both. Along with these, the "hermits" interact with and have fun with each other, by having build collaborations, doing challenges, occasional wars and just goofing around.
