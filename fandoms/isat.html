In Stars and Time

Overview

In Stars and Time is an video game about a party of heroes about to fight the final boss. They go through the local village one last time before going into the boss arena, and.. die immediately? This game is a timeloop game that confronts the mental and emotional aspect of being stuck in one (and a half) days.
