Hello Charlotte

Overview

Hello Charlotte is an rpg-maker game series wherein you take the role of a Puppeteer guiding the game's titular protagonist, Charlotte. You and your puppet traverse through surreal and sometimes horrifying worlds, such as a realm made entirely of meat, a library born of your mind, and school.
