Worm

Overview

In a world where people gain superpowers from traumatic experiences, Taylor, a 14 year-old girl with the power to control insects, wants to become a hero to escape from her pretty stinky life. Of course, everything immediately goes wrong when she accidentally saves a local group of villains from a rivaling group, and now they think she is a villain as well and gives her an invitation to join.

Worm is very long so it's kinda hard to get into, but the story flows pretty well and the writing quality is pretty consistently good throughout, which is nice for a long web serial. I really like how thought out and built the world is, with superpowered peoples tackled on a realistic angle.
