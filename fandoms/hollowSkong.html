Hollow Knight / Silksong

Overview

Hollow Knight and Hollow Knight: Silksong are metroidvania games where you play as a little bug and traverse through a really mean stinky world. In both games you fight a variety of enemies and bosses, and try to figure out what happened to the vast ruined kingdom that you find yourself in, and if there is any way to save it.

Hollow Knight is pretty hard and it makes my fingers hurty, which is good because hard things are fun. I haven't been able to play Silksong, but I've heard it's marginally harder than its predecessor. The games may be hard, but they're also fair and give you tools and gear to politely execute your enemies with. The games also just looks gorgeous, and I like how you can break a lot of the background objects because that's funny. Great experience and fun, if you like pain. - Xian
