Critical Role

Overview

Critical Role is a channel on Youtube that upload their Dungeons and Dragons campaign on the internet. The campagin players and Dungeon Masters are all popular voice actors. They also have a patreon where they allow members to watch their episodes live, and also hold live shows during local Comicons.
