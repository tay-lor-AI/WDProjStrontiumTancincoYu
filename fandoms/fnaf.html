Five Nights at Freddy's

Overview

FNAF is a game series where you have to survive the night against multiple animatronics. Most of the games have you take on the role as a security guard, and you have to last five nights at Freddy's security office. The series has very deep lore, and there are multiple books that accompany these games and add to the story.

Growing up, it was funny to watch what lengths Mr. MatPat had to go through to understand the series' storyline, with details and hints being very obscure and Scott Cawthon, the creator of the series, changing and retconning story elements all the time. The gameplay (up until Ultimate Custom Night, where for me the fun factor goes down a lot) is also very fun, having to stay in one place and use whatever tools you have to survive. - Xian
