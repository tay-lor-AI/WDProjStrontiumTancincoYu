So I'm A Spider, so What?

Overview

Wakaba Hiiro is a Japanese student who gets reincarnated in another world as a spider in the Great Elroe Labyrinth, basically the most dangerous place in the new world that she resides in. It is very unlikely that she will survive in this world, but using her human intellect and new spider abilities, she might be able to survive.
