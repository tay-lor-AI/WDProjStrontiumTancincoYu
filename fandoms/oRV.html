Omniscient Reader's Viewpoint

Overview

Kim Dokja is a young man leading a simple, yet sad life, who has been the sole reader of a novel, Three Ways to Survive in a Ruined World, alternatively translated as Ways of Survival, for 13 years of his life. On the day the final chapter was published, reality and fiction began to merge, the world as he knew it becoming the world of the story.
